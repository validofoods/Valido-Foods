Baked Lemon Pepper Chicken
Season chicken with lemon pepper, basil, oregano. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes until golden and juicy.
PoultryLemon Pepper30 Min
+
Ingredients
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 lemon juiced (1/4 cup)
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 teaspoons Valido's Lemon Pepper
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons Valido's Dried Oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly grease a 9x13 baking dish.
  2. Lay chicken breasts in the baking dish. Drizzle with lemon juice and rub with olive oil.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon pepper, basil, oregano, and salt. Season chicken with mixture, coating all sides.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, until chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.
  5. Serve hot. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and lemon slices (optional).

Chicken Fried Rice
A quick weeknight classic made with Valido Fried Rice Seasoning, diced chicken, veggies, and scrambled eggs.
RiceFried Rice SeasoningQuick
+
Ingredients
  • 3 skinless chicken breasts (boneless)
  • 12 ounces teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Valido's Fried Rice Seasoning
  • 3 tablespoons sesame oil (or vegetable oil)
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots (thawed)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 2 eggs (slightly beaten)
  • 3 cups cooked white rice (day old or leftover cold rice is best)
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
Instructions
  1. Cook chicken covered in teriyaki sauce and Valido's Fried Rice Seasoning in a crock-pot on high heat for 3 hours, or in oven at 375°F for 35 minutes. Once cooked, slice into small pieces. Set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the peas/carrots mix, onion, and garlic. Stir fry until tender.
  3. Lower the heat to medium-low and push the mixture off to one side. Pour eggs on the other side of skillet. Stir fry until scrambled.
  4. Add the rice and soy sauce and blend all together well. Stir in cooked chicken and cook until mixture is heated through. Serve and enjoy!

Maple Glazed Salmon
Pan-seared salmon with a sweet maple glaze and Valido Chili Powder. Ready in under 20 minutes.
SeafoodChili Powder20 Min
+
Ingredients
  • 3 pounds salmon
  • 3 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons Valido's Chili Powder
  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1½ tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1½ teaspoons salt
  • ½ cup pure maple syrup
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Line a large rimmed cookie sheet with foil.
  2. In a small bowl, stir together the mustard, chili powder, smoked paprika, brown sugar, and salt to form a thick paste.
  3. Place the salmon on the foil and spread the entire fish with the mustard paste using the back of a spoon.
  4. Pull the foil up and over the salmon (don't lay foil on the paste). Pinch closed like a packet.
  5. Bake for 14–15 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven, carefully open foil (watch for steam). Turn oven to broil.
  7. Drizzle maple syrup over the salmon. Place back under broiler for 2–3 minutes.
  8. Remove and serve immediately.

+

Foil Cajun Chicken & Veggies
One-pan foil packets with seasoned chicken, peppers, and zucchini. Bake at 450°F for 20–25 minutes.
PoultryCajunOne Pan
+
Ingredients
  • 2–3 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 large zucchini, chopped
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon each: Valido Garlic, Chicken, Cajun, Paprika, Oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon Valido Black Pepper (optional)
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Cut 4 sheets of foil into ~12"×12" rectangles. Set aside.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Spoon mixture evenly onto the 4 foil sheets. Fold and seal foil.
  3. Place foil packs on a baking sheet and bake 20–25 minutes. Be careful when opening — steam is trapped inside.

Valido's Overnight Chicken

PoultryCurry PowderMeal Prep
+
Ingredients
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • ½ cup honey
  • ⅓ cup dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Valido's Curry Powder
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
Instructions

Creamy Broccoli (Keto)
Low-carb creamy broccoli side dish seasoned with Valido Garlic Powder. Keto-friendly comfort food.
KetoVeggiesGarlic
+
Ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 5 cloves garlic (minced or crushed)
  • ½ cup bacon (4–6 slices, chopped — optional)
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • ½ teaspoon Valido Garlic Powder
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup shredded Mozzarella
  • ¼ cup Parmesan cheese (optional)
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. In a large pan over medium-high heat, cook chopped bacon for 2–3 minutes or until almost crispy.
  2. Remove bacon. In the same pan, add butter, garlic, broccoli, Valido Garlic Powder, crushed red pepper, and a dash of salt and pepper. Sauté for 2–3 minutes until garlic is fragrant. Add heavy cream, cheese, and cooked bacon.
  3. Transfer to preheated oven and cook for 12–15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and broccoli is done to your liking.

Creamy Parmesan Garlic Shrimp Pasta
Rich, creamy pasta with garlic shrimp and parmesan, seasoned with Valido Garlic and Seasoned Salt.
PastaSeafoodGarlic
+
Ingredients
  • 1 pound jumbo shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • ½ teaspoon Valido's Seasoned Salt
  • Pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon Valido's Garlic Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ cup parmesan cheese
  • 8 ounces linguine (cooked al dente)
Instructions
  1. In a medium skillet, add olive oil. Add shrimp and garlic, cook over medium-high heat until almost pink but not fully cooked.
  2. Add heavy cream, chicken broth, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, and parmesan cheese. Stir until smooth. Simmer 3–5 minutes until sauce thickens. Add pasta and stir to coat.
  3. Garnish with fresh parsley, shredded parmesan, and lemon wedges.

Healthy Baked Parmesan Chicken Nuggets
Baked, not fried. Crispy parmesan coating with Valido Black Pepper. Kids love them.
PoultryBlack PepperKid-Friendly
+
Ingredients
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts (cut into pieces)
  • ½ cup parmesan cheese (divided in half)
  • ¼ cup cold water
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon Valido's Black Pepper
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with foil or parchment paper.
  2. In a food processor, pulse chicken breasts, ¼ cup parmesan, cold water, salt, and Valido's Black Pepper for 1 minute until minced and combined.
  3. Mix panko breadcrumbs and remaining ¼ cup parmesan in a shallow dish. Shape chicken mixture into small nuggets and coat with the breadcrumb mix. Place on baking sheet and drizzle with oil.
  4. Bake 12–15 minutes, then broil 2–3 minutes until golden. Serve warm with your favorite dipping sauce.

+

Taco Salad
Fresh taco salad with seasoned beef, cilantro-lime dressing, cheese, and optional tortilla strips.
MexicanSaladFresh
+
Ingredients
Dressing
  • ⅓ cup light sour cream
  • ⅓ cup low-fat yogurt
  • ½ cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon Valido Garlic Salt
  • ½ teaspoon Valido Taco Seasoning
Salad
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 red bell pepper (diced)
  • ½ teaspoon Valido Chili Powder
  • ¾ cup salsa
  • 6 cups romaine lettuce (chopped)
  • 2 green onions (sliced)
  • ¾ cup Mexican blend cheese
  • 2 tomatoes (cut into wedges)
Instructions
  1. For the dressing: combine all dressing ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Spray a large non-stick skillet with cooking spray. Sauté diced pepper 3–4 minutes. Add beef and chili powder, cook 5–6 minutes until browned. Stir in salsa and cook 1 minute. Remove from heat.
  3. Assemble: place 1½ cups lettuce on each plate. Top with ¾ cup meat mixture. Add green onions, 3 tablespoons cheese, and 3 tablespoons dressing. Garnish with tomato wedges.

Valido's Taco Spaghetti
A fusion twist — spaghetti meets taco seasoning for a quick, kid-approved dinner.
PastaMexicanKid-Friendly
+
Ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 lb extra-lean ground beef (90%+)
  • 1 carton (32 oz) beef broth
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) fire roasted crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (4.5 oz) chopped green chiles
  • ½ cup water
  • 3 tablespoons Valido's Taco Seasoning
  • 12 oz spaghetti (broken in half)
  • 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
Instructions
  1. In a 5-quart Dutch oven, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add beef and cook 5–7 minutes, stirring frequently, until no longer pink. Stir in broth, tomatoes, chiles, water, and Valido's Taco Seasoning.
  2. Add spaghetti and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer. Cook uncovered 15–20 minutes, stirring frequently, until pasta is tender. Top with cheese and serve.
